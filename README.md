# Ascend — the roadmap to your dream job

**Ascend is "LeetCode for every career."** Pick a company and a role, and get a
structured, NeetCode-style preparation roadmap that takes you from *"I want this
job"* to *"I'm ready to interview."*

🔗 **Live demo:** https://bschwar9.github.io/leetcode-for-finance/

---

## The idea

Preparing for a specific job today means stitching together a dozen tabs — one
site for interview questions, another for salaries, LinkedIn for networking,
scattered YouTube videos for the actual skills. Ascend brings that entire
journey into **one place, organized by company and role**.

Search for a role — say, *Financial Analyst at Morgan Stanley* — and Ascend
lays out everything you need to maximize your chances, across five focused tabs.

## What each role gives you

| Tab | What it does |
|-----|--------------|
| 🗺️ **Roadmap** | An ordered, NeetCode-style topic tree with curated video/resource links, so you study the right things in the right order — and check them off as you go. |
| 💬 **Interview Questions** | Crowd-sourced, real interview questions organized by round and difficulty. Upvote what's useful and add your own. |
| 🤝 **Networking** | People in your target role you can reach out to, with ready-to-send outreach message templates. |
| 💰 **Compensation** | Self-reported base / bonus / signing ranges so you know your worth before you negotiate. |
| ✅ **Skills & Requirements** | The must-have skills, nice-to-haves, and certifications that actually move the needle. |

The prototype is focused on **finance**, with seeded examples for Morgan Stanley
(Financial Analyst), Goldman Sachs (Investment Banking Analyst), JPMorgan
(Quantitative Analyst), and BlackRock (Risk Analyst).

## Status

This is an early **front-end MVP** built to demonstrate the concept. All content
shown is illustrative sample data.

## Roadmap

- **Backend + accounts** so questions, votes, and progress are shared across all
  users rather than stored locally.
- **Live data integrations** for networking (LinkedIn) and real compensation data.
- **More tabs:** resume / ATS keyword review, mock interviews and practice
  problems, an application tracker, and company-culture reviews.
- **Beyond finance:** expand into software, consulting, accounting, and more.
